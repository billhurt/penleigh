01 About

A specialist firm. Not a generalist.

For over a decade we’ve placed senior talent in the midstream and subsea inspection markets — the niche corners of energy where the right person is rarely on the market, and the wrong hire is genuinely expensive.

We work on a retained basis only. That means we’re partners — not a CV-blasting agency. Every search is led directly by James Leigh, with no junior handover, no off-the-shelf longlists, and no incentive to fill roles quickly at the cost of fit.

Our network spans 6,000+ contacts across 21 countries, built up role-by-role in pipeline services, subsea inspection, pipeline construction, NDT and adjacent fields. When you’re hiring for a role that genuinely matters, that depth of network is the difference.

James Leigh Founder & Director · Leeds, UK

03 Services

Two ways to work with us.

Both engagements are retained. Both are led personally. The difference is what you need at the moment we start a conversation — to fill a critical role, or to understand the talent landscape before you hire.

i.

Retained Executive Search

For C-suite, senior management, sales and operations roles where the right candidate is rarely actively job-hunting. We headhunt, qualify, behaviourally assess, and shortlist — typically delivering candidates 3–5 weeks from brief.

Every shortlist is delivered through our HireInsight platform: video introductions, psychometric profiles, competency results and references in one secure place.

ii.

Market Mapping

Before you hire, know the field. We produce structured intelligence on the talent landscape for a defined role, function or region — current incumbents, comp benchmarks, mobility signals, and the people you should be talking to in 12 months’ time.

Used by founders, boards and PE investors planning a build, a restructure or an acquisition.
