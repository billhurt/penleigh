02 Before you write

A few common questions.

How quickly will I hear back?

James responds personally to every enquiry, usually within a working day. If you’ve flagged something time-sensitive in your message, sooner.

Do I need to commit to anything to start a conversation?

No. Initial scoping calls are always confidential and never charged for. We only formalise the engagement once we’ve both decided it’s the right fit.

Can you help if I’m not sure whether I need a search yet?

Yes — that’s often where the most useful conversations begin. We can talk through the role, the market, and whether a full retained search, a market map, or simply a few introductions would serve you best.

What if my role isn’t in midstream or subsea?

We’re honest about scope. If your role sits outside our markets we’ll say so directly, and where we can we’ll point you toward a specialist who’ll do better by you than we would.

Is everything we discuss confidential?

Always. We treat every enquiry — including ones that don’t turn into engagements — as commercially sensitive. NDAs are standard for mapping work and available on request for searches.
